About Us

Safe&Aware is a non-profit organization to teach safety, self-defense, and confidence to women and youth. We have reached out to many communities and domestic abuse shelters to educate and support victims in addition to organizing events in Chicago and its suburbs to raise awareness about the importance of self-defense and how it can protect people from potential threats
