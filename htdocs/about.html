About Us

Safe&Aware is a non-profit organization to teach safety, self-defense, and confidence to women and youth. We have reached out to many communities and domestic abuse shelters to educate and support victims in addition to organizing events in Chicago and its suburbs to raise awareness about the importance of self-defense and how it can protect people from potential threats.

Rhea Makkuni
Founder, Blogger

Hello! My name is Rhea Makkuni, and I’m a junior at Walter Payton College Preparatory. When I was young, during a trip to India I saw the impact of domestic violence on individuals and families, and the pain, suffering, and long-term consequences it brings. The trauma, fear, and isolation experienced during an abusive relationship can have a lasting impact on an individual's mental well-being and also on their children. To provide relief to the immense suffering and emotional distress survivors go through, I hope to provide them with the necessary support and resources to heal and recover.
